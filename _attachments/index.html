Searching

Default search on sample name:

  • "ofhc copper"
  • copper AND ofhc
  • copper OR lead
  • coppe?
  • cop*

Searches are case-insensitive and OR by default.

You can specify a different search field:

  • experiment:majorana
  • technique:(gamma OR naa)
  •    AND name:plastic

These are the supported fields:

  • grouping/experiment
  • name
  • description
  • id
  • source
  • owner
  • institution
  • technique
  • requestor
  • practititoner
  • isotope

About

Persephone is a software package for storing measurements of material radiopurity. It was originally created by James Loach at LBNL.

Contributors: Keith Adler, Matthew Bruemmer, Jodi Cooley, Adam Cox, James Loach, Khang Nguyen, Alan Poon, Benjamin Wise.

Supporting institutions and collaborations: Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory (LBNL), LBNL Low Background Facility (LBF), the MAJORANA Collaboration, Southern Methodist University (SMU), Shanghai Jiaotong University (SJTU).

This work was supported by the National Science Foundation under Grant Number 1242640 and the Assays and Acquisition of Radiopure Materials (AARM) collaboration.
